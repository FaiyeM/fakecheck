# FakeCheck — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-24_

FakeCheck ("we", "the app") helps you photograph an item and get an AI-assisted
opinion on whether it appears authentic. This policy explains what we collect,
why, and how long we keep it. We designed FakeCheck to require **no account and
no personal information**.

## What we collect

- **Photos you take or select** of the item you want checked. These are uploaded
  to our secure storage so our vision models can identify and assess the item.
- **An anonymous device identifier** generated on your device, used only to group
  your scans and apply fair-use rate limits. It is not linked to your name,
  email, phone number, or any advertising identifier.
- **Scan results and any corrections you submit** (your stated outcome plus an
  optional written explanation and up to three supporting photos), used to
  improve the accuracy of the service.
- **Basic technical data** such as app version and platform (iOS/Android).

We do **not** collect your name, email, address, contacts, precise location, or
advertising identifiers, and we do **not** sell your data or use it for ads.

## How we use it

To identify the item, run authenticity checks, show you a result, and improve our
models over time. Correction photos are re-encoded on our servers, which removes
all embedded metadata (including any GPS/location EXIF data) before they are
retained for model improvement.

## How long we keep it

- **Scan photos** are automatically deleted within **30 days**.
- **Corrections** (and their EXIF-stripped supporting photos) may be retained
  longer because they are used to improve model accuracy; they contain no
  personal identifiers.

## Sharing

We use third-party infrastructure providers (cloud hosting, object storage, and
AI vision providers) solely to operate the service. They process images only to
deliver FakeCheck's functionality and under their own security obligations. We do
not share your data with anyone else.

## Your choices

You can clear your local scan history in the app at any time. Because no account
is required, photos are tied only to the anonymous device id. To request deletion
of server-side data, contact us at the address below.

## Important note

FakeCheck provides an **AI-assisted opinion, not a guarantee** of authenticity.
Always use your own judgment for purchase or resale decisions.
